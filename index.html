Crystal Gatherer!

How to Play the Game:

  1. Each time the game starts or restarts, you will be given a randomly generated "goal" score.
  2. Click one of the four crystals below to gather crystal points.
  3. You win by matching your gathered score to your goal score.
  4. You lose if your gathered score goes above your goal score.
  5. The gather score of each crystal is hidden, until you click the crystal
  6. Each time the game starts or restarts, the gather score value of each crystal will change.
